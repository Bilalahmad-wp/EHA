From the hottest new looks in balayage hair color to various types of voluminous hair extensions, our stylists specialize in today’s top hairstyling trends! We’re home to some of the most talked about stylists in South Florida, and our family of clients come from all over the region to experience the best of the best in specialty services. We take pride in our work, and we take pride in our clients.

The staff at Executive Hair have gained a reputation for being industry insiders and innovators. They have trained and educated themselves in some of today’s top looks before social media influencers blew them up. Trend-forecasting is our thing. And we love working with clients who want to recreate the latest styles! Not to mention, they're more than happy to pass on styling tips and hair product recommendations for the longevity of your new hair. So, don't be shy to pick their brain during your complementary image consultation.

If you didn’t notice already, we’re obsessed with the power of transformation. In fact, we’re masters of the art of makeovers! Our Executive Hair Additions stylists love working with each client’s specific taste and favorite trends. We focus on finding your inner beauty and pulling together a hairstyle that is a reflection of it! Whether your ideal look is beachy waves, natural highlights, or waist-length extensions, our stylists passionately work to achieve a look that is an Addition to your personal style! Our vast skill set allows us to give each client beautiful hair with its own set of individualized dimensions. We create more than just a hairstyle, but a work of art!

We want to make sure that you leave the salon absolutely glowing! Even if you're just dropping in for a bang cut, face frame highlights, extension stitch, or a regular trim, our Executive Hair Additions team makes sure that you enjoy everything that our luxury salon atmosphere has to offer. There’s no such thing as service that’s too small, and we’re dedicated to solving all your beauty needs in our prioritized sessions! After all, Executive Hair Additions was built to give our clients the ultimate treat yourself experience. Our mission is to make a lasting change in our clients’ lives by showing them what a difference it can make when you take time to invest in your beauty and celebrate yourself and your hair.
