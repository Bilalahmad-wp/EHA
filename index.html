About Us

About
Executive Hair Additions

At Executive Hair Additions, our craft is our passion! Our master stylists have committed their careers to learning and perfecting the industry’s latest techniques to better serve their clients. Your every hair wish is our command, and we’re confident that we have the skill set to execute any look and inspiration that you might have.

Executive Hair Additions was founded by a team of entrepreneurial hairstylists who were hungry to learn every facet of the beauty industry. It’s their passion and their dedication to making clients look and feel beautiful.

From the hottest new looks in balayage hair color to various types of voluminous hair extensions, our stylists specialize in today’s top hairstyling trends! We’re home to some of the most talked about stylists in South Florida, and our family of clients come from all over the region to experience the best of the best in specialty services. We take pride in our work, and we take pride in our clients.
